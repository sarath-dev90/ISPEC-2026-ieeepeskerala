Extra Charges

One of the authors (typically the presenter) is responsible for paying the extra page charges and/or the extra paper charges, if applicable. These charges must be paid through the author’s registration page.

Extra Page Charge

For final camera-ready full papers, pages exceeding the standard 6-page limit will incur an extra page charge of JPY 15,000 per page. A maximum of two extra pages are allowed, bringing the total to no more than 8 pages. Please note that no papers exceeding 8 pages will be accepted.

Extra Paper Charge

Each non-student registration covers the publication of up to two full papers, while each student registration covers one full paper. Authors who wish to publish full papers exceeding these limits must pay an extra paper charge of JPY 30,000 for each additional paper.

Cancellation Policy & Refunds

  • All refund requests must be submitted via the contact form.
  • All cancellations received before 15 August 2026 (23:59 JST) will incur a JPY 5,000 cancellation fee.
  • Refunds for cancellations received after 15 August 2026 (23:59 JST) will NOT be issued, even under special circumstances. Refunds due to visa issues will NOT be accepted.
  • All refunds will be processed after the conference and will be issued to the original payment method.
  • Payment platform transaction fees are non-refundable.
  • Registrations cannot be transferred or shared.
